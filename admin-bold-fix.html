📋 How to use this panel
  • Content box: For books — type or paste your blurb. For free stories — paste the full text.
  • Status: Published, Coming Soon, Fighting Back, Genesis, A Dark Thought, or Free.
  • Tags: Comma separated, no # needed.
  • Cover images: Use the uploader at the bottom. After upload, click Use as Cover to drop it straight into the cover box.
  • After changes: Click Publish to GitHub — updates the Manifest and saves your changes automatically.
