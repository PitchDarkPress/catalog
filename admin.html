📋 How to use this panel
  • Content box: For books — type or paste your blurb. For free stories — paste the full text. Use the toolbar to format.
  • Status: Published, Coming Soon, Fighting Back, Genesis, A Dark Thought, or Free.
  • Tags: Comma separated, no # needed.
  • Cover images: Use the uploader at the bottom. Enter the filename in the book (e.g. covers/my-book.png).
  • After changes: Click Download Updated Catalog and upload to GitHub as index.html.
